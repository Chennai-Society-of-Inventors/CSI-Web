What we Do?
Chennai Society of Inventors, abbreviated as CSI, is a newly formed student driven NGO which focuses on humanitarian engineering. At CSI, we primarily help in connecting problems faced by communities with engineering students across Tamil Nadu who in turn apply their engineering skills to solve them. We then aid them in designing, analyzing and solving the various problems and challenges faced by the poor, marginalized and under-developed communities in our villages and towns in Tamil Nadu using their technical skills. We are committed in bringing out new inventions for the benefit of public.
